Other Issues
Skin
Acne
  • Common — mention to patients before starting
  • Manage with standard acne treatment
  • Not a reason to stop lithium
Hair thinning
  • Less common — more likely at higher doses
  • Treatment: minoxidil
  • Consider dose review if significant and levels allow
Psoriasis
Ask about psoriasis history before starting lithium. Lithium can worsen existing psoriasis and less commonly may trigger new onset.
  • If psoriasis flares after starting lithium — refer to dermatology
  • Stopping lithium is not automatic — dermatology guides that decision
  • Co-management is usually possible
Calcium / parathyroid
Mechanism and prevalence
~4%
High estimate for clinically significant hypercalcemia on lithium
  • Lithium shifts the calcium setpoint upward — parathyroid gland recalibrates
  • Results in mild hypercalcemia and elevated PTH
  • Usually asymptomatic
  • Symptomatic hypercalcemia — fatigue, kidney stones, constipation — warrants prompt referral
