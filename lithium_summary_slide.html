Lithium in primary care

SUMMARY

01

One of the most effective medications in treatment-resistant depression — guideline-based, yet chronically underutilized

02

Unique benefits — recurrence prevention, suicide reduction, possible neuroprotection — that outweigh its risks when used carefully

03

A large number of our patients are potential candidates — we see them longitudinally, across years and episodes

04

The decision takes time and a relationship — one we already have. We can acquire the skills and build a support culture.
