Safer prescribing — if needed
  • Dispense smaller quantities
  • Trusted person in the patient's life holds the medication
  • Same approach used with benzodiazepines, opioids, antihypertensives, warfarin
The primary care perspective
  • You know this patient — longitudinal relationship, established rapport
  • You are not meeting them for the first time
  • Clinical judgment applies — this is not a protocol decision
Lithium is the only mood agent with replicated evidence of suicide reduction. Most lithium toxicity is survivable with good management. Lethal outcomes are generally secondary to severe CNS effects with subsequent cardiovascular collapse.
Lithium exposure data: Gummin DD et al. 2023 Annual Report of the National Poison Data System (NPDS). Outcomes: moderate in 1,290 cases, major in 167 cases, 7 deaths among 3,324 single exposures. Acetaminophen figures: CDC estimates; ~250–350 intentional fatalities annually. Acetaminophen available without prescription in any quantity.
