Lithium also reduces likelihood of the next episode — NNT~3 for recurrent unipolar prevention (Davis 2006); HR 0.31 in Finnish registry (Tiihonen 2017).
Caveat: maintenance effect may require levels of 0.6–0.75 mEq/L. Target 0.4 in elderly or frail patients.

To reach 3,400–12,600 potential responders, lithium needs to be offered to all 8,600–21,000 eligible TRD patients.
