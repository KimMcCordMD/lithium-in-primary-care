Migraine caution: some data suggest lithium may paradoxically worsen both migraines and mood response in certain patients — statistically significant, uncertain clinical significance. Start very low, monitor both headache and mood. Stay at OTC or very low prescription doses until no clear harm signal. Shared decision-making throughout.
Suggested starting dosing — migraineurs, nervous starters, DIYers
  • Week 1–2: ½ tablet of 5mg orotate (~12mg carbonate equivalent) — watch for any change in headache pattern
  • If no headache signal: advance to 1 full 5mg tablet (~25mg carbonate equivalent) — wait another 2 weeks minimum
  • Reassess: any mood signal? Sleep improvement? Headache change? Then discuss next steps together
  • No level required at these doses — TSH and creatinine sufficient
Patient voice
  • "The dose that works for me is about 50mg lithium carbonate." — patient on long-term lithium, felt blunted at standard doses, settled at 50mg with sustained signal
For patients who want to try before committing to a prescription — OTC orotate is a legitimate starting point. The biology does not require a prescription dose to begin.
